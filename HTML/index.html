Welcome to my Bio!

My name is Troy Delacruz. I am a twenty-six year old African American who has ambitions to learning and understanding how technology works.
I've always had a strong desire to learn how to program but never had the chance due to mental health issues.
Luckily with my amazing support system I know now that those days are behind me and my desire to learn has grown much more stronger and hungrier.
I am very much a beginner in this field of work but I will continue to learn everything there is to know to achieve my dreams and earn my keep!
